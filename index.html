Some of the problems that we can help with

Time-series Forecasting

For many businesses, it is critical to understand and forecast what will happen in the future. What word does the user intend to write next? Will tomorrow's stock price drop? How much inventory should we order for the next season? The answer is in your data!

Personalized Recommendation

Your customers often give signals about what they like. By analysing these signals it is possible to identify preferences of each customer. This in turn allows to accurately show your customers the content they’ll love, thus drive significant engagement and revenue.

Document Searching

Need to discover clusters of documents? Maybe map relationships between different data points? Or would you just want to drill down to the results you're looking for? We can assist you in preparing your data to maximize the relevance of search results while keeping real-time performance.

Performance Analysis

Define a problem. Prepare the data. Apply an approach and get closer to your goal. But is that enough to guarantee success? You could spend a lot of time choosing, running and tuning algorithms. But, it is important to also evaluate the utilized algorithms and discover which change is effective and which isn't.
